Bomonti by rotana:

Bomonti Residences by Rotana is a luxury residential project located in Istanbul’s vibrant Bomonti district, one of the city’s most dynamic and culturally rich neighborhoods. Modern apartments feature elegant designs, panoramic city and Bosphorus views, and high-end comfort with spacious layouts and high-quality finishes. Residents enjoy a wide range of premium amenities, including a fully equipped fitness center, swimming pool, sauna, landscaped gardens, and 24-hour security. The project is ideally located close to Nişantaşı, Taksim, and Bomontiada, offering both convenience and a lively urban lifestyle with easy access to shopping, dining, and cultural attractions. MTR-Interior proudly contributed to the development of this prestigious project. Our team ensured that the interiors combine style, functionality, and quality, creating a truly luxurious and comfortable living experience for every resident.

Mall of istanbul residence:

Mall of Istanbul High Residence is a distinguished luxury residential project in Istanbul’s Başakşehir district. Modern apartments feature elegant designs, high-quality finishes, and spacious layouts ranging from 80 m² to 679 m². Residents enjoy premium amenities including restaurants, cafés, spa, fitness center, and direct access to the Mall of Istanbul with international brands and entertainment venues. The project is strategically located with direct metro access and easy reach to Istanbul Airport, offering both convenience and a sophisticated urban lifestyle. MTR-Interior proudly contributed to the development of this prestigious project. Our team ensured the interiors combine style, functionality, and comfort, creating a luxurious living experience for all residents.
